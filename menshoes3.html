Description

Released as a competitive basketball shoe in 1987 when sneaker culture was kicking off, the Slipstream Lo was then reissued in the 2000s and gained a reputation as the “The Beast” thanks to its crazy colourways. These iconic kicks have been reimagined and updated for the sneaker fanatics of today, while staying true to their basketball heritage. Embrace this classic shoe and celebrate the modern twists added by its varied leather textures and moulded TPU eyestay.
